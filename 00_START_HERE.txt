âœ… BONDING INTERFACE SUPPORT
â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•â•

The agent now correctly reports bonding interface speeds as the SUM of all 
active member speeds. For example:

  Bond with 2x 100 Gbps members = 200 Gbps reported in SNMP

This is done by:
  1. Querying VPP sw_interface_bond_dump for bond info
  2. Querying sw_interface_slave_dump for member details
  3. Summing speeds of all active (non-down) members
  4. Reporting aggregate speed in SNMP OID 1.3.6.1.2.1.31.1.1.1.15
